NEHA BAGEPALLI

El Monte Educate

A website designed to provide resources for low income families from the city of El Monte - specifically for children, to support their educational progress as well as creating a portal for parents to connect with students and access updates about school easily.

Timeline

September - December 2023

My Role

UI/UX Designer

Toold Used

Figma

The Problem

Children from low income backgrounds are at a disadvantage to gain additional support in their education. Additionally, school websites can contain overwhelming amounts of information and have a high cognitive load that not every parent or family can digest.

Target Audience

With a focus on developing a solution for low income families in El Monte, I expanded on this description of the group to define them as:

  1. Parents of children aged 5-11, who live in El Monte, make less than $40,000/ year, and have a foreign language as a first language.
  2. Educators aged 30-50 who teach in El Monte public elementary schools and make more than $60,000/ year.
  3. Children aged 5-11 who attend public elementary school in El Monte, whose household income is less than $50,000/ year.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

With a goal of creating a resource page for families, I observed patterns in existing school websites. While conducting this competitive analysis, I found that most pages had a heavy cognitive load and could be very hard to digest for many people.

Problem Statement

How might we develop a resources website that benefits students and parents in low income families in El Monte?
